# Goldman Sachs – Risk Management Virtual Experience Program

[![Forage](https://img.shields.io/badge/Forage-Job%20Simulation-blue)](https://www.theforage.com/simulations/goldman-sachs/risk-ljdz)

This repository documents my completion of the **Goldman Sachs Risk Management Job Simulation** on Forage. The program provided a practical, hands-on introduction to how risk analysts at Goldman Sachs identify, categorize, and mitigate risk — spanning everyday decision-making, individual client credit assessments, and real estate investment analysis.

## About the Program

Goldman Sachs' risk exposure comes from a wide range of counterparties across its **Global Banking and Markets (GBM)** and **Asset and Wealth Management (AWM)** divisions. This simulation walks through the core skills a risk analyst uses to evaluate credit quality and support informed, strategic decision-making.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Risk identification and classification (Market, Credit, Liquidity, Operational, Model risk)
- Quantitative, weighted credit risk scoring
- Scenario-based risk impact analysis
- Probability-based risk assessment and mitigation strategy selection
- Applied use of Excel for financial risk modeling
